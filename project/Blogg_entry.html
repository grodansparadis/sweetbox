Uploaded a new version of the schemas for the sweetbox. Most parts are in place now. Missing is power for the RGB LED array because I don't know how many I can fit in to the $400 budget at the moment. I will try to make a new video also in a free days showing the progress.

I have not been able to determine if I should support other sound then talk for the audio of the SweetBox. My decision depends on how much juice I can get out of the PIC32. This is why the schema has two different versions of the filter section for the audio at the moment. One with a cut of at about 4000 Hz and one at 21 KHz.

Added a pressure sensor to be able to record barometric pressure.
